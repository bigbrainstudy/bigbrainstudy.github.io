about bigbrainstudy.github.io

Paying for information sucks. In the 21st century, it has become easier and cheaper than ever to access a myriad of information, yet many educational resources are still locked behind paywalls and subscriptions for no good reason.

That's why bigbrainstudy was created: to offer students and educators complete resources, assignments, and tutorials for free! No payment, no subscription - just free, unfettered access to the information we all desire to have.
